## Overview

"The Sealed Nectar" (Arabic: الرحيق المختوم, Ar-Raheeq Al-Makhtum) is a highly acclaimed biography of the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) written by Sheikh Safiur Rahman Mubarakpuri. It was honored with the first prize by the Muslim World League in a worldwide competition on the biography of the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) held in 1396 H (1976).

The book provides a detailed account of the Prophet's life, from his birth and lineage, through the period of revelation and preaching in Mecca, the Hijra (migration) to Medina, the establishment of the Islamic state, various battles, and up to his passing.

## Key Features

*   **Comprehensive Coverage:** It covers almost all aspects of the Prophet's life with significant detail.
*   **Authenticity:** The author relies heavily on authentic sources of Hadith and historical records.
*   **Chronological Order:** Events are presented in a clear chronological sequence.
*   **Accessible Language:** While scholarly, the language is generally accessible to a wide readership.

It has been translated into numerous languages and is considered a staple resource for anyone wishing to study the Seerah.
