**Details of the Event:**

The Messenger of Allah (peace and blessings of Allah be upon him) stayed with Banu Amr ibn Awf in **Quba** for over ten nights. During this time, he laid the foundation of the mosque that was **"founded upon piety"** (referencing Quran 9:108), and he prayed in it.
