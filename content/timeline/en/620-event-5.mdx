## The Prophet's ﷺ Marriage to Sawdah bint Zam'ah

After the death of Umm al-Mu'minin **Khadijah bint Khuwaylid (may Allah be pleased with her)**, who was among Allah's great blessings upon the Messenger of Allah ﷺ, the Messenger of Allah ﷺ married **Sawdah bint Zam'ah ibn Qays ibn Abd Shams ibn Abd Wadd ibn Nasr ibn Malik ibn Hisl ibn Amir ibn Lu'ayy, the Qurayshiah**.

He married her in Mecca before the Hijrah. She was among those who embraced Islam early and migrated the second migration to Abyssinia. Her husband was **As-Sakran ibn Amr**, who had embraced Islam and migrated with her. He died in Abyssinia or after returning to Mecca.

When her waiting period ended, the Messenger of Allah ﷺ proposed to her and married her. She was **the first woman he married after the death of Khadijah**. She was in her **sixties** at that time.

---

## She Gifted Her Night to Aisha (may Allah be pleased with her)

When the Prophet ﷺ intended to divorce her, she reconciled with him by **gifting her turn (her night) to Aisha (may Allah be pleased with her)**, so he kept her. He did not have any children from her until he ﷺ passed away.
