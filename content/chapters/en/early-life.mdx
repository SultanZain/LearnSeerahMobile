# The Miraculous Beginning

The Year of the Elephant (Am al-Fil) is a pivotal year in Islamic history, approximately 570 CE. It is famous for the failed invasion of Makkah by Abrahah al-Ashram, the ruler of Yemen, and more importantly, for the birth of Muhammad ﷺ.

## The Army of the Elephant

Abrahah built a magnificent church in Sana'a and wanted to divert the pilgrimage from the Kabah to his church. When his plan failed, he led a powerful army, including war elephants, to destroy the Kabah...
