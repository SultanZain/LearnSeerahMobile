The **Sahabah** were the direct companions of the Prophet Muhammad (Peace Be Upon Him).
This term refers to individuals who:
1. Saw or met the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) in person.
2. Believed in his prophethood.
3. Died as Muslims.

They played a crucial role in preserving and transmitting the teachings of Islam, including the Quran and the Sunnah (Prophetic traditions). Their understanding and practice of Islam are considered highly authoritative by Muslims.
